## üí° Pro Tips

### Email Best Practices
1. **Send Tuesday-Thursday 9-11am** (best open rates)
2. **Personalize the first line** (mention something specific about their site)
3. **Keep it short** (under 150 words for cold outreach)
4. **One clear CTA** ("Are you open to a 15-min call?")
5. **Follow up 3-7 days later** (50% of replies come from follow-ups)

### Using the Talking Points
1. **Before calls:** Review relevant messaging framework
2. **During calls:** Have app open on second screen
3. **After calls:** Add detailed notes immediately
4. **Practice:** Read objections responses out loud

### Pipeline Management
1. **Daily:** Check follow-up reminders
2. **Weekly:** Review pipeline metrics
3. **Monthly:** Analyze what's working
4. **Always:** Update status immediately after interactions

## üéì Common Questions

### Q: How many leads should I contact per day?
**A:** Start with 10-15 per day. Quality over quantity. Research and personalize each one.

### Q: When should I follow up?
**A:** 
- First follow-up: 3-4 days
- Second follow-up: 7 days
- Third follow-up: 14 days
- Break-up email: 30 days

### Q: Which business types are easiest to sell to?
**A:** 
1. Healthcare/Pharmacy (highest compliance pressure)
2. Large retailers (highest lawsuit risk)
3. Subscription services (clear ROI on conversion improvements)

### Q: What if I don't have their contact email?
**A:** Use tools in Knowledge Base:
- LinkedIn Sales Navigator (best for roles)
- Hunter.io (find email patterns)
- Apollo.io (comprehensive database)

### Q: How do I personalize the emails?
**A:**
1. Visit their website, find a specific accessibility issue
2. Mention their business type specifically
3. Reference recent news/funding/growth
4. Customize pain points to their situation

### Q: What's the average sales cycle?
**A:**
- Small companies: 2-4 weeks
- Medium companies: 4-8 weeks
- Large enterprises: 8-16 weeks

### Q: Should I call or email first?
**A:** Email first to warm them up, then call as follow-up. Cold calls work but are harder.

## üî• Advanced Tips

### A/B Testing Your Outreach
Test these variables:
1. **Subject lines:** Risk vs. Value vs. Curiosity
2. **Email length:** Short (100 words) vs. Medium (150 words)
3. **CTA:** Call request vs. Free audit vs. Case study
4. **Send time:** Morning vs. Afternoon
5. **Personalization level:** Generic vs. Highly specific

Track results in your notes field!

### Warming Up Cold Leads
Before emailing:
1. Follow their company on LinkedIn
2. Engage with their content (like/comment)
3. Connect with their team members
4. Mention this in your outreach

### Creating Urgency (Ethically)
- "I'm doing free audits for 3 pet e-commerce companies this month"
- "ADA lawsuits in pet industry increased 40% this quarter"
- "Limited availability for onboarding before Q1"
- "Special pricing for companies who commit by [date]"

### Multi-Threading
Don't rely on one contact:
1. Start with primary contact role
2. If no response after 2 touches, try secondary contact
3. Connect with multiple stakeholders
4. Someone will engage eventually
